Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish that consists of rice with veggies, eggs, and other sources of protein stir fried together usually with some soy sauce and other seasonings. It's a cheap dish that is easy and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • MSG
  • Veggies
  • Some sort of protein if desired
  • Green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. On a hot wok, fry the rice with some soy sauce and msg.
  3. Add your vegetables, eggs, and protein.
  4. Season to taste, should be quick.
