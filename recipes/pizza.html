Pizza

Description

To make the dough, you must mix flour, water, yeast, oil, salt, and sugar until combined. Let rest for 24 hours. To make the sauce, heat up canned tomatoes with salt and sugar. Heat oven and put cheese and sauce on dough. Bake pizza

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, oil, salt, and olive oil until dough is formed.
  2. Heat up tomatoes with salt and sugar.
  3. Stretch dough into circle or square and put sauce and cheese on it
  4. Heat in hot oven until cooked.
