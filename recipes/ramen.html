Ramen

Description

Ramen is a common dish originating from Japan. It is usually known to be a cheap college dish bought at the store. But given some time, it can be a vibrant dish.

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Broth
  • Eggs
  • Veggies
  • Protein

Steps

  1. Boil Broth
  2. Put eggs and veggies and ramen in broth.
  3. Season to taste.
